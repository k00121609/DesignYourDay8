What is this 'Design Your Day' Website About?

This website is about 'Mindfulness' for health a key feature of 'Lifestyle Management.' It is an integrated source all in one website, for many life-management tools. There are pages here for: Health, Organisation, Stars and Stories, Games. It is an interactive website and there is something for everyone here.

The 'Health' page has articles, information and links to popular topics in society related to health with a holistic approach. Here this approach includes not only medically therapeutic factors, but the current 21st Century approach to health care called Up-Stream Factors. These are about a persons overall lifestyle approach to health, what is their: social context, environmental, are they getting important often not thought of elements like are they getting enough sleep, their diet, fresh air.

This culture shift in emphasis on lifestyle and a holistic, integrated, synergistic approach to health is evidenced in popular opinion, seminars, circulars. In particular, the The RSA Awards (student design awards) resources include TED Talks which are at the cutting edge of societal trends and a very good indicator of in-person audience. The focus of these are on a holistic approach to healthcare in particular. These factors consider a holistic approach to healthcare, that are not only directly therapeutic, but also in equal importance, consider a person's, social, environmental, lifestyle context.

From the RSA Brief 2 'The Good Life,' I have sought in this website to achieve their criteria of: to inform, educate, entertain, through information, various interactive elements and gaming which the criteria is a design to have mainstream appeal and be something that could be part of popular culture.
