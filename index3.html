- Diary Journals & Trackers -

Diary journals and trackers have been with us since the beginning of time in a creative multimedia way, from cave dwellers drawing wall images to represent their lives, to map-making planning to their presence in films and documentaries. In film, they have featured as life and story-telling tools in such movies as: The Notebook, Dracula, Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets, Mean Girls, Bridget Jones Diary, Cruel Intentions and Harriet The Spy.

In the past people regularly kept paper journals and trackers. Nowadays, similar is achieved using Blog’s, however in popular culture there has been a return to pen and paper diaries. In this tutorial to the left, I have added a layer to this, where in addition to their paper journal trackers, they can bring the tracker aspects of these to life in using Microsoft Excel through instructions that are animated.

I think the significance of these type of recordings is summed up in Oscar Wilde’s quote, “Memory… is the diary that we all carry about with us.”
